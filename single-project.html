Competitive Coding Workshop

This is a handson workshop on basic coding concepts there will 3 teaching sessions 2 on day 1 and 1 on day 2, followed by a contest. There are no prizes for winners, it is just for your practice. Attendance will be provided only to students who attend the sessions.

What will be covered in the session?

Day 1

  • Basic Maths for CP
  • Recursion and basics of Dynamic Programming
  • Sorting and Binary Search

Day 2

  • Strings and arrays (prefix sums, sliding window)
  • Greedy and Dynamic programming
  • 3hr coding contest

Date: Nov 19, Nov 20

Time: 10am to 4pm

Venue: A102, A107 labs

Fees: 100Rs.

Organized By: Turing Hut

Who can attend: 2nd year CSE and CSBS students

Type: Competitive Coding Workshop
